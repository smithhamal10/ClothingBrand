CLIENT SERVICES

What is the return policy?

+

NEPKIT offers returns within 14 days of purchase. Items must be unworn and in original condition with tags.

How can I track my order?

+

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can also track it through your account dashboard.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, NEPKIT ships globally. Delivery times and shipping rates may vary depending on your region.

How do I contact customer support?

+

Feel free to reach out via our Contact page. We're here to help you!

Can I change or cancel my order?

+

Orders can be changed or canceled within 2 hours of placing them. Please contact support immediately if needed.
